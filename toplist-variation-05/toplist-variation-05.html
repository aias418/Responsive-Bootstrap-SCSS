Canada’s Best Sports to Bet On

Betting fans in Canada enjoy gambling on a huge variety of sports in 2021, but there are eight that always prove particularly popular online. Here we’ve listed the best sports to bet on in Canada:

NHL Hockey Betting

Canadians love hockey - that's a universal truth! The NHL is the only major Canadian-facing sport to get exposure across the sportsbook industry worldwide. You’ll have the opportunity to bet on your favourite team at most Canadian sports betting sites.

NFL Football Betting

The NFL is hugely popular around the world but especially in North America, with Super Bowl betting being a huge draw. Many Canadians also enjoy following games all season in the National Football League.

NBA Basketball Betting

The NBA franchise is a power to be reckoned with. Bettors in Canada will be aware of the huge opportunities for betting when it comes to basketball, with local team the Toronto Raptors competing in the Atlantic Division.

UFC Betting

UFC has seen a huge surge in popularity in recent years and is a firm favourite with bettors in Canada. Take advantage of a great range of betting options, with over 40 events and hundreds of fights scheduled each year.

CFL Football Betting

CFL is another national favourite. Throughout the season, fans can enjoy placing their bets on the nine competing teams, with a great range of markets to choose from.

Soccer Betting

For Canadian sport fans, soccer betting is extremely popular. In Canada, you might want to bet on Major League Soccer (MLS) or the Canadian Premier League (CPL). Additionally, with international tournaments such as La Liga, the English Premier League and of course the World Cup, online sportsbooks offer tons of soccer betting markets to Canadian bettors.

College Football Betting

Football fans in Canada may not be as familiar with placing bets on college football games. The U Sports Football games are the top level of Canadian college-level play, with 27 teams competing. Also, with US-based associations like the NCAAF churning out some of football’s biggest future stars, it’s definitely worth a watch.

Horse Racing Betting

Horse racing has a rich heritage and events take place worldwide which you can bet on. Canadian bettors can put money on horses in the Canadian Triple Crown, which is Canada’s most popular horse racing tournament domestically. Internationally, it’s also worth looking into The Grand National (UK) and the Dubai World Cup (UAE).
