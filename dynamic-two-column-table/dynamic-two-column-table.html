Pennsylvania Online Gambling

With the signing of HB 271 by Gov Tom Wolf in 2019, residents in Pennsylvania can partake in online casino gaming, poker play as well as sports betting. The Pennsylvania Gaming Control Board regulates and audits all state-wide casino operations. It is responsible for the licensing as well as assisting problematic gamblers.

Delaware Online Gambling

In 2012, online gambling in Delaware was legalized and became fully regulated by the Delaware Division of Gaming Enforcement. Online casino games such as slots and table games, online poker, sports betting, and fantasy sports betting are all legal within the state border. However, there are several restrictions in place especially for online casinos, thus the options are quite limited for some activities.

Nevada Online Gambling

In Nevada, while it is legal to visit a land-based casino to play games of chance such as slots, it is prohibited to do so online. Online casino activities are only allowed if they are categorized as games of skill such as poker and online betting. Formed in 1955, the Nevada Gaming Control Board is responsible for licensing and enforcing regulations as well as ensuring that all casino and gambling operators are paying their taxes.
